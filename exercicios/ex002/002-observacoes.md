## Delimitação da tag PHP (5 maneiras)
1. **``` <script language="php"> ... </script> ```** (Funcionada no PHP5)
2. **```<?php ?>```** --------------------------------------> Super Tag PHP
3. **```<? ?>```** -------------------------------------------> Short Open Tag
4. **```<% %>```** ------------------------------------------> ASP Tag
5. **```<?= ?>```** -----------------------------------------> Short Tag PHP
